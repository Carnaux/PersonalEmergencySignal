What it is

P.E.S is a system that helps you on the traffic in cases of emergency.

It is a mobile app, a desktop app and a device.

How it works

In case of emergency you use the mobile app to send an emergency notification to the nearest hospital.

There they recieve the notification and informations about where you are and how long would take you to arrive. All displayed on the desktop app.

After the hospital send a confirmation, your mobile app will be able to activate the P.E.S device in your car.

After the P.E.S activation

The device will send signals to the cars around you and notify the drivers that there is someone near them that has an emergency.
